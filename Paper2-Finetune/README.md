# Thai Banana Cultivar Dataset

A public image dataset containing 35,000 images of 10 Thai banana cultivars for object detection and classification research.

---

## Motivation

This dataset was created to support the conservation of Thai banana cultivars and to facilitate AI-based agricultural research. It aims to provide a benchmark dataset for classification and detection tasks involving Thai banana varieties under both natural and recaptured image conditions.

---

## Collection

The dataset contains 15,000 images collected from 10 Thai banana cultivars.

Classes include:

1. Nak banana
2. Ngachang banana
3. Candy apple banana
4. Hom Thong banana
5. Hua Amua banana
6. Kai banana
7. Nam Wa Dam banana
8. Lep Chang Kut banana
9. Thepphanom banana
10. Nam Wa banana

Image acquisition sources:
- Direct camera-captured images
- Monitor-recaptured images

Images were collected using smartphone cameras and monitor display recapturing under controlled and natural lighting conditions.

---

## Bias

The dataset may contain biases related to:
- lighting conditions
- camera devices
- geographic sampling limited to Thailand

These factors may affect model generalization.

---

## Ethics

All images were collected for academic research purposes. No personal or sensitive information is included. The dataset does not contain human subjects.

---

## Limitation

- Limited to 10 Thai banana cultivars
- Images mainly collected in controlled environments
- May not generalize to unseen cultivars or international banana varieties
