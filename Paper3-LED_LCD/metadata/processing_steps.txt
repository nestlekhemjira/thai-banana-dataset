DATA ACQUISITION AND PROCESSING PIPELINE

This dataset contains two domain conditions:

1. Original Camera Domain
- Images captured directly using standard cameras under controlled conditions
- Represents high-quality visual conditions captured under controlled settings

2. Screen Recapture Domain (LED/LCD)
- Images captured by photographing displayed content on digital screens using smartphones
- Two display types involved:
  - LED display condition
  - LCD display condition
- These conditions introduce different display characteristics and visual artifacts
- Designed to study domain shift effects and potential moiré patterns caused by differences in display technologies between LED and LCD screens

3. Data Processing
- Preprocessing steps applied across both domains including noise reduction and resizing
- Standard resizing for model training to a 1:1 aspect ratio (640x640 resolution)
- Pixel value normalization applied consistently across all images
- Annotation verified for format consistency and bounding box validity
- Management of negative samples (images with empty bounding boxes) to improve background-to-object separation and model robustness in non-object regions

4. Dataset Split Strategy
- Dataset split into two parts based on a standard ratio: 90% for model training/validation and 10% for model testing (held-out evaluation set)
- Split performed at image level, ensuring no overlap between training and test sets
- Split applied consistently across both domains to ensure balanced representation
